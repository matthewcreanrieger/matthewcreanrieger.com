Kanji And Kana Master

Kanji and Kana Master is an iOS app developed and designed to make learning Japanese simple, straightforward, and fun.

It uses the principles of gamification to engage users and make a complex language easy to learn. What also differentiates Kanji and Kana Master from other Japanese language learning apps is that it includes all 2,136 common-use Kanji characters.

It is free to download on the App Store and has over 1,500 downloads and 250 active users. Check it out on the App Store.
