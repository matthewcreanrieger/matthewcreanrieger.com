Pilot44 Internship

Pilot44 is a digital innovation outpost that accelerates the innovation strategies of corporations. The internship was in conjunction with Miami University's San Francisco Digital Innovation co-op program.

The initial role was operations management but quickly evolved to fulfill a variety of business needs. Expanded responsibilities included organizing events and managing client relationships. By the end of the internship, responsibilities included creating and leading presentations for major clients.

Three examples of presentations created are a CPG Investment Insights presentation for a multi-billion dollar company, a flash research presentation to help an international client prepare for a large investment, and a BotDay convention recap for the Pilot44 team.
