Digital Innovation Co-Op at Pilot44

What is Pilot44?

Pilot44 is a digital innovation outpost that accelerates the innovation strategies of corporations. My internship took place during the 2016 fall semester and was in conjunction with Miami University's San Francisco Digital Innovation Co-Op program.

My Role

I was initially hired into an operations management role. By the end of my internship, I was both creating and leading presentations for major clients. Additionally, I helped organize events and manage client relationships. My internship was unique because my role evolved to fit whatever need Pilot44 had.

Contributions

Three examples of presentations I created are a CPG Investment Insights presentation for multi-billion dollar company, a flash research presentation to prepare an international client for a large investment, and a BotDay convention recap for the Pilot44 team.
