TAG Foot Showers

The charge for this group project was to provide a go-to-market strategy for TAG, a high-end, hydrotherapy foot shower designer.

Research led to the conclusion that a B2B strategy was preferable to a direct-to-consumer approach. A three-step strategy to sell to industry wholesalers by initially targeting trade shows was delivered in our final presentation. Additionally, data collected from Usability Hub tests revealed a clear user preference between two existing TAG logos. Responsibilities included presenting, creating the PowerPoint, formatting deliverables, and writing half of the appendix.

The presentation was commended for its unique approach to the problem and for the fact that it was the only presentation that proposed a B2B sales approach.
