Mirror's Edge Digital Branding

The task was to individually complete a digital brand analysis and action plan for Mirror's Edge, an action-adventure platformer in which the player acts as a courier in a dystopian world and utilizes parkour to escape capture.

The analysis of Mirror's Edge's digital brand included an online branded community assessment, a real-time marketing analysis, a social influence analysis, a social media tone and voice analysis, and a digital creative strategy brief. The video associated with this brief can be viewed on this webpage or on my YouTube channel.

While this was an entirely theoretical project in a classroom setting, the instructor encouraged its use as a portfolio piece, citing its impressive depth and level of creativity.
