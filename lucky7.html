Blackhorse Bourbon: Entrepreneurship Capstone

The assignment was to develop a launch strategy for a new line of bourbon from Lucky 7 Distillery, a startup in Batesville, Indiana.

Individual responsibilities in the group included formatting the PowerPoint, presenting, creating the branding strategies, and creating the promotional video.

Brand development was not requested, but ultimately served to be the most crucial element of the presentation for the client. Following the project's conclusion, an offer was presented to continue working as a part-time freelance video content creator for the distillery.

A letter of recommendation from the capstone instructor, a former consulting Director at Deloitte and former president of a publicly traded company, is available upon request.
